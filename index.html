THE UNIT


125th Assault Corps was born on 27th of March 2020 by members with over 10 years experience in the ARMA community. We are a StarSim unit that offers our members an enjoyable time. We are an Infantry Corps heavily focused on the hard and fast attack to overwhelm the enemy whether it be through conventional infantry assaults or use of shield tactics, chances are if you want it, we’ve got it!

Our operations are on Fridays or Saturdays depending on the week, we operate on a rotational basis to allow our members to attend as many ops as possible, the standard operation time is 7 pm BST.
With the launch of this website we wish 125th a happy birthday! First of the many to come!
